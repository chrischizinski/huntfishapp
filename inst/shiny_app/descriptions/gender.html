Summary:
Bars are filled to indicate percentage of female and male customers relative to the total number of customers purchasing in a given time period.

Grouping Variables:
 The proportion of female and male customers is calculated separately for each group. By default, the item year is included as a grouping variable. If the reporting period is monthly, then month is included as an additonal grouping variable. If the reporting period is daily, then the issue date is included as a grouping variable. The subplot variable drop down menu can be used to specify additional grouping variables.

 Tips:
To remove "(Missing)" group, activate filter by selecting all values. For example, check boxes for "Female" and "Male" on gender filter to remove entries with missing values for gender attribute.

  Caution:
Customers may purchase multiple item types and therefore the sum of the number of customers by item type may be greater than the total.
