About:
The goal of huntfishapp is to provide state and provincial fish and wildlife agencies and non-governmental organizations with a web-based, exploratory data analysis application for hunting, fishing, and outdoor recreation sales data.

Authors:
 The development of the code and functionality of the app was primarily developed by Nat Price. The app is currently being maintained by Chris Chizinski. Additional authors of the app include: T.J. Fontaine, Kevin Pope, Jeff Rawlinson, and Micaela Rahe. A manuscript describing the app is available here.
