Summary:
Revenue is calcuated as the sum of the price attribute for all items.

Grouping Variables:
The revenue is calculate separately for each group. By default, the item year is included as a grouping variable. If the reporting period is monthly, then month is included as an additonal grouping variable. If the reporting period is daily, then the issue date is included as a grouping variable. The subplot variable and color variable drop down menus can be used to specify additional grouping variables.

 Tips:
To remove "(Missing)" group, activate filter by selecting all values. For example, check boxes for "Female" and "Male" on gender filter to remove entries with missing values for gender attribute.

By default, all subplots will have the same scale. The y-axis scales can be changed on the "Options" tab.

 Caution:
The revenue calculation is simply the sum of the price attribute and therefore may or may not include additional fees depending on how the price attribute is defined in the database.
